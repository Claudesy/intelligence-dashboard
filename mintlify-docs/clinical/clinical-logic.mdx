## Diagnosis Flow

1. **Symptom collection** — Chief complaint + vital signs + patient history
2. **Red flag detection** — Hardcoded vital sign thresholds (no AI needed)
3. **Candidate retrieval** — Hybrid keyword + semantic search from 159 KKI diseases
4. **LLM reasoning** — Structured differential diagnosis via DeepSeek / Gemini
5. **Validation** — Cross-check with clinical guidelines
6. **Output** — Ranked differentials with ICD-10 codes and confidence scores
