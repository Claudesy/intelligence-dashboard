# AI Governance

AADI implements responsible AI practices for clinical decision support.

<Note>
  For the full AI governance policy, see the internal [AI_GOVERNANCE.md](https://github.com/sentra-healthcare/abyss-monorepo/blob/main/app/primary-healthcare/dashboard/docs/AI_GOVERNANCE.md).
</Note>

## Key Principles

- **AI assists, never replaces** — All diagnoses require clinician confirmation
- **Transparency** — Confidence scores and reasoning shown for every suggestion
- **Red flag safety net** — Critical vital signs trigger alerts without AI
- **Fallback chain** — DeepSeek → Gemini ensures availability
- **Audit trail** — All AI interactions logged via Langfuse
